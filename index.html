PROJECT ATLANTIS

Preparing Citizens with Flood Risk Information

Finalists of Aquahacking BC 2020

Canada has the world’s largest coastline and a vast system of rivers. This makes the risk from flooding a serious threat to Canadian communities. However, the access to flood risk data is not easy. For some communities, it is just not available. The challenge remains in finding, understanding and updating this information. We are working to provide open access to the existing flood risk records and developing new ones.

Our Project

Communication

Understanding flood risk is too important to be ignored. We work to present flood risk data to the public in a user-friendly manner.

Mapping

A core component to our services is the digital mapping of flood basins in an open interactive interface. Our services extend to flood plain profiling through cheap image rendering technologies.

Simulations

Taking the existing information and models, we work to create simulations and forecasting models that not only help understand flood risk, but also monitor for potential climatic or geological events that can cause flooding.
